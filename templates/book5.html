The Story of Jett

The Story of Jett is set in a distant corner of our galaxy on the moon Trident, one of the swirling satellites of the planet Neptuna. It is the story of an eight-year-old boy's mission to find his long-lost family and will thrill younger readers who enjoy adventure books or listening to bedtime stories read to them by adults.

Fully illustrated in gloriously vibrant colours by the highly acclaimed artist, Susy Jones, this is a must read for all youngsters who relish exciting tales, vibrant characters and stories with plenty of surprises.
