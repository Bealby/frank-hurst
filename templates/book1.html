The Postmistress of Nong Khai

Mike Rawlin, an ambitious Customs Intelligence officer, is posted to the British Embassy in Bangkok, where he is tasked with infiltrating a dangerous band of drug smugglers in the infamous and remote Golden Triangle. Passion, deception and intrigue provide a heady mix in this fast-paced crime thriller set in London and Thailand. A highly entertaining read, The Postmistress of Nong Khai is full of fascinating and elusive characters and will appeal to readers who enjoy being kept guessing until the very end.

Hugely Enjoyable

A sheer delight of a read. Intriguing plot with believable characters, locations you can smell and taste and a climactic finish which leaves you craving for more. A sequel is a must, as Mike Rawlin still has unfinished business.

Really Very Good Indeed

Initially an impulse buy this novel by Frank Hurst is totally engrossing and gives a vivid insight into the world of international drug smugglers and those whose job it is to catch them. This tale captures the essence of the chase, together with a highly charged love interest leaves you longing to pick up the sequel. Well done Mr Hurst.
