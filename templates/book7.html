The Story of JETT - The Quest Begins

The second book in the exhilarating Story of Jett series sees ten-year-old Jett embark on an expedition to find his parents. His quest, set on a remote moon in a distant part of our galaxy, is full of intrigue and his journey soon becomes perilous in the extreme.

To triumph, he must be brave and resourceful. He will need all his newly learned espionage skills if he is to defeat the dangerous forces railed against him. They will do all in their power to stop him. But as he tackles each new dangerous challenge, he discovers that hitherto sworn enemies can be kinder than he imagined. They are prepared to lend a hand too.
