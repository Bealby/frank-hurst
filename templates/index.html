The Shrouded Coast

Frank's latest novel in his 'Hansom Cab' series is now finished.

Set in Victorian England, 'The Shrouded Coast' sees Henry Gough travel to remote Cornwall where he encounters a veritable web of intrigue and mystery involving a gang of vicious smugglers, strange hauntings, murder and revenge.

The scene is set for another thrilling adventure which will test Henry's courage and resolve, and bring him to the brink in more ways than one...

Publication is scheduled for later this year.
